This is my favourite walk on the island. You leave the marina heading north, and within 10 minutes the tourist infrastructure drops away completely.

## The route

Follow the coastal track past the Acantilados viewpoint, continuing on the PR-TF-57 path towards Punta de Teno. The path is well-marked but rocky underfoot — proper shoes, not sandals.

At the lighthouse you'll see all three western Canary Islands on a clear day. The walk back uses the same path; there's no loop option.

## What you need

- Water: minimum 1.5 litres per person, no water sources on route
- Start before 9am in summer — the last section has no shade
- Sun protection is essential; this coast catches full western sun from midday

## Difficulty note

Mostly flat to moderate incline. One short rocky descent into a dry barranco. Fine for anyone with reasonable fitness.
