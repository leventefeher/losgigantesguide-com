Whether you are an absolute beginner or an experienced dancer already, join Baila Canarias in Puerto Santiago for their friendly group classes. Get active, learn to dance, and make some new friends!

## What to expect

Weekly group classes in the open air, in a relaxed, friendly environment. Students of all ages with no previous experience needed, just check the schedule and rock up! No partner is needed, but it always helps the balance if you bring one with you 💃🏻

## Practical tips

- Message on WhatsApp to make sure the class is on, as they are weather dependent
- Wear comfortable shoes and clothes, bring some water or grab a drink from the bar
- Classes are usually 10€, with discounts for couples or booking multiple classes
