**Tipsy Yoga!**

Join our very fabulous Dee on Monday and Thursday mornings at 9 am by our poolside for a morning of pure relaxation and re-energising.

Suitable for all ages and levels (mats available).

Afterwards, you can head up to our lovely terrace and enjoy one of our scrumptious breakfasts and delicious coffees!

Tipsy Yoga makes Monday a beautiful way to start your week. Price is 5€.

## What to expect

Yoga for all levels and ages by the poolside to start the morning.

## Who it's for

Everyone is welcome, most people bring their own Yoga Mats, but Dee has some spares if you don't have one.

## Practical tips

- Always best to call ahead to check it's on, especially in questionable weather
- Wear something you can move in and bring a light layer for the end
- Morning sessions are cooler and calmer than evening ones
