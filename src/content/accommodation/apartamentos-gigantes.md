A solid choice for longer stays. The complex is quiet (no entertainment programme, no pool bar — genuinely peaceful), the management is responsive, and the apartments are kept in decent condition.

## What's good

- Full kitchen: hob, oven, fridge-freezer, washing machine in two-bed units
- Pool is cleaned daily and never feels overcrowded
- 5-minute flat walk to the marina and main supermarket
- Free parking in the complex

## What to know before booking

Studios are compact — comfortable for a couple, tight for two weeks with suitcases. Book a one-bedroom if you're staying more than a week.

Ask for an upper-floor apartment when booking. Ground floor units face the car park.

## Longer stays

They offer a weekly discount from 7 nights and a monthly rate for 28+ days. Worth asking directly rather than going through booking platforms.
