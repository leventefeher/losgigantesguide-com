Brand voice & tone

Personality

A knowledgeable friend who lives in Los Gigantes. Warm, honest, and direct. Never a corporate travel brand.

Audience

British tourists, long-stay visitors, expats. Aged 35–65. Want real tips, not TripAdvisor consensus.

Tone

Conversational, specific, trustworthy. Uses real details (times, prices, insider tips) rather than vague praise.

SEO focus

Lead with "Los Gigantes" (70% of searches). Add "Tenerife" in subheadings and body copy, not always in titles.

Write like this

  • Go at 8am — the 10am trip is packed
  • The black sand gets very hot by noon
  • Ask for the off-menu catch of the day
  • The evening dance class runs Tues & Thurs

Avoid this

  • A truly unforgettable experience!
  • One of Tenerife's hidden gems
  • World-class dining awaits you
  • Don't miss this amazing opportunity
